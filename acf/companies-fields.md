# Companies Field Group

The Companies field group stores organizational and business-related information used throughout the CRM.

## Location Information

* setor-emp
* endereco-emp
* cidade-emp
* estado-emp
* pais-emp

## Contact Information

* e-mail-emp
* representante-emp
* responsavel-emp

## Business Classification

* grupo-emp
* relac-emp
* geracao-emp

## Products and Solutions

* produtos-emp
* soluc-emp

The `soluc-emp` field is used as a grouped solution selector and may contain:

* Software (sw)
* Hardware (hw)
* Services (servicos)

## Relationship Fields

* agente-emp

This field is used to establish relationships between records and support advanced filtering, search, and reporting features.

## Purpose

This structure allows companies to be categorized, filtered, exported, and connected to contacts through relational queries and custom CRM workflows.
