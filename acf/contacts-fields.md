# Contacts Field Group

The Contacts field group stores people-related information associated with company records.

## Personal Information

* email-ctt
* sexo-ctt
* endereco-ctt
* cidade-ctt
* estado-ctt
* pais-ctt

## Professional Information

* empresa-ctt
* departamento-ctt
* cargo-ctt

## Events and Participation

* forum-ctt
* waf-ctt
* palestrante-waf-ctt
* academy-ctt

## Research and Surveys

* pesquisa-ctt
* tipo-pesquisa-ctt

## Experience Tracking

* anos-waf-ctt
* anos-palestr-ctt

## Webinar Participation

* quais-webinares-ctt

## Relationship Fields

* agente-emp

This field references company records and enables relational searches, reporting, filtering, and data exports.

## Purpose

The Contacts structure was designed to support CRM operations, event participation tracking, survey management, relationship mapping, and advanced reporting capabilities.
